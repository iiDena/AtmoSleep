Chronotypes

Learn about your own chronotype


Bear
Score 16-24
If you scored between 16-24 in the test you are a bear!
Bears tend to follow the natural solar cycle, you don't have much trouble sleeping or falling asleep. you typically get 8 hours of sleep. Bears get very tired if they don't get their usual amount of sleep hours, but overall have much energy during the day and most likely to be an exrovert.

Here is a suitable schedule for you

7-8am - Wake up

10am-2pm - Best time to focus on work

2-4pm - Do something you enjoy

4-10pm - Socialize or complete smaller tasks

10-11pm - Get ready for bed

11pm-7am - Sleep

Dolphin
Score 25-34
If you scored between 25-34 in the test you are a Doplhin!
Dolphins are known to be light sleepers, and can often be the type to be diagnosed with a sleep disorder such as insomnia. You most likely have a hard time falling asleep and waking up. You tend to be a very creative person and achieve succes.

Here is a suitable schedule for you

8-9am - Wake up

8-10am - Selfcare

10-12am - Simple tasks

12-4pm - Productive time! Best focus and more energy during this time

4-10pm - Time so relax

10-12 Get ready for bed without any distractions

12-8am - Get as much sleep as possible

Lion
Score 35-46
If you scored between 35-46 in the test you are a lion!
Similar to the bear, you function well during early hours. You enjoy waking up early and getting a lot done before lunch, but just like a lion all that energy goes away fast. You don't miss an opportunity to nap and recharge for the day. Lions are usually seen as leader due to their early habits, you also like to stay active.

Here is a suitable schedule for you

6-7am - Wake up

8-12am - Focus on work, these are your best focus hours

12-4pm - Socialize or workout

4-6pm - Get in a quick snooze

6-10pm - Relax and get ready for bed

10pm-6am Sleep

Wolf
Score 47-60
If you scored between 47-60 in the test you are a wolf!
Wolves are known to be productive at night, you need time to snooze in the morning to sustain your energy and creativity later in the day. You don't get much done until the sun sets and your most likely happy to sleep at midnight or even past. Wolves tend to be more introverted and reserved unlike the other types.

Here is a suitable schedule for you

9-11am - Wake up

11-1pm - Get your day started with light tasks

1pm-5pm - Do something you enjoy

5-8pm - Creative or more intense tasks

8-12pm - Night routine and relax

12-2am - Sleep
