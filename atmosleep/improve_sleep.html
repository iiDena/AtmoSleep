10 tips to improve sleep

1. Regularity. Consistency

To improve your sleep, you need to have a routine. Your brain has a build in clock, and it seeks consistency and a routine. By waking up and going to sleep around the same time each day will help your brain understand when you should sleep and when you should wake up.

2. Darkness

Some studies show that reducing light 2 hours before sleep can help improve your sleep. Light usually indicates that it's daytime which makes your brain stay active and not seek sleep. It's recommended that 2 hours before sleep that you stop using your phone device or tv and dim the lights around you.

3. Avoid noise

Loud noises can make it hard to sleep but at the same time inconsistent quiet noises can make it hard to sleep too, while consistent white noise helps, the sound of rain or some relaxing atmospheric sounds

4. Walk it off

Don't stay in bed for too long if you can't sleep. Your brain should associate the bed with sleep. That's why you should take a walk.

5. Keep it cool

The recommended to keep the bedroom is around 18 degrees Celsius. It is shown to be the perfect temperature to give you the best possible sleep.

6. Avoid alcohol and caffeine before sleep.

It's recommended to avoid caffeine and alcohol 6 hours before sleep and other studies say that you should avoid it for at least 2 hours before sleep. Caffeine and alcohol have shown to boost brain activity and keep you awake and of course too much alcohol can cause a bad hangover.

7. Relax before you sleep

Have a small routine to help you relax before you sleep. This will set the mood and help you get your mind off work and daily stress before sleeping.

8. Stay active during the day.

Being active and working out has shown to improve concentration and focus. Using your energy helps you relax and feel tired.

9. Avoid carbs before sleep.

Food a lot of high carb food right before sleep boosts your energy which can make you feel more energized for a short time. This can ruin your sleep and make your body want to stay active instead of relaxing when you try to sleep.

10. Relaxation techniques.

Yoga or meditation can help improve your sleep and make you more mindful. Being aware of your emotions and breathing can help you focus on relaxing body and mind.
